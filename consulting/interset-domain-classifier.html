DGA Threat Detector

At Interset (now OpenText), I developed a classifier to detect domain-generation algorithm malware in live log streams—achieving 90% accuracy and enabling real-time threat mitigation.

Executive Summary

  • Delivered a DGA detection model with 90%+ accuracy, improving incident response times for malware events.
  • Processed billions of DNS requests in real time, flagging malicious domains before they impacted operations.
  • Packaged the solution into a production-ready service, demonstrating immediate ROI through reduced false positives.

Technical Overview

  • Streaming Architecture: Ingested DNS logs via Apache Kafka, performed feature extraction (n-gram and entropy metrics) with Apache NiFi.
  • Modeling & Deployment: Trained Random Forest classifier on labeled DGA vs. benign domains, exported as PMML for low-latency inference.
  • Integration & Monitoring: Deployed prediction service in Docker containers, integrated with SIEM dashboards for live alerts and analytics.
