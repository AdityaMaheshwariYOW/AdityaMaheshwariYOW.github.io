Airport Screening Demand Predictor

Partnered with CATSA to forecast the proportion of passengers requiring screening across major Canadian airports, reducing wait times and optimizing resource allocation.

Executive Summary

  • Improved passenger experience by predicting screening demand within 5% error across peak and off-peak hours.
  • Enabled dynamic staffing models, reducing passenger wait times by up to 20% at key checkpoints.
  • Delivered actionable forecasts to operations teams via automated dashboards and alerts.

Technical Overview

  • Data Integration: Joined live flight schedules, boarding-scanner logs, and historical throughput data using SQL and Python ETL scripts.
  • Modeling: Applied non-linear regression and gradient-boosted trees (xgboost) to predict screening rates by terminal and time-of-day.
  • Deployment & Monitoring: Containerized pipeline with Docker, scheduled via Apache Airflow, and visualized in Tableau with custom KPI alerts.
