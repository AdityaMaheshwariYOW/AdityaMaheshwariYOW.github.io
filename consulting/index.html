Private Sector

After my co-op at Interset, I realized data science thrives as iterative contracts—you tune models, let them run in production, then refine. Here are a few engagements where I drove real-world impact for clients.

Airport Screening Demand Predictor

Partnered with CATSA to forecast the proportion of passengers requiring screening across major Canadian airports, reducing wait times and optimizing resource allocation.

Twitter Sentiment Extraction Pipeline

Modernized Advanced Symbolics’ sentiment analysis by migrating a script-driven Twitter pipeline into Apache Airflow—automating workflows and ensuring reliable ML model retraining.

DGA Threat Detector

Built a classifier for Interset (now OpenText) to detect domain-generation-algorithm malware in log files—deploying a contract-based solution that ran in production and iteratively improved over time.
