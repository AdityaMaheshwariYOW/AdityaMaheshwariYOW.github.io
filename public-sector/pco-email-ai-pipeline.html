Inbox AI Assistant

At the Privy Council Office, I built a scalable AI pipeline to parse ~10,000 daily emails to the Prime Minister—transforming raw messages into actionable insights for government communications.

Executive Summary

  • Developed a robust ML workflow to classify topics and sentiment in ~10K PMO emails daily, enabling rapid policy insight.
  • Presented outcomes at Code for America Summit 2024, demonstrating government-grade AI to public-sector peers.
  • Established reusable infrastructure and best practices for future ML initiatives across federal departments.

Technical Overview

  • Data Ingestion & Preprocessing: Automated email retrieval via secure API, preprocessed text in Databricks/Spark (tokenization, stop-word removal).
  • Modeling & NLP: Utilized BERTopic for dynamic topic extraction; fine-tuned HuggingFace transformers for sentiment classification.
  • Deployment & Monitoring: Packaged inference as Azure Functions, scheduled retraining via Azure ML Pipelines, visualized results in PowerBI with custom alerts.
