Public Sector

In government roles, I’ve had the chance to understand massive citizen–policy feedback loops and spotted gaps in existing frameworks. All the problems are about turning citizen feedback into actionable policy insights, while also understanding the long timeline for change, navigating large organizations, and translating technical solutions for non-technical stakeholders.

Quality of Life Hub

Led development of Statistics Canada’s user-facing Quality of Life Hub: transforming raw social data into interactive dashboards that give Canadians and policymakers real-time insights into well-being trends.

Inbox AI Assistant

Designed and deployed an open-source AI pipeline at the Privy Council Office to parse the Prime Minister’s public inbox and pull topics and sentiments. Helped showcase how cutting-edge machine learning can scale citizen feedback and build trust in government communications.

Auto-filing taxes for people on social assistance

Working in a $10M innovation fund, we had an opportunity highlight solutions for citizens who did not owe tax, but could claim benefits by filing. By mining micro-data from all Canadians over the past 40 years, we found patterns in populations who had unclaimed benefits, and accordingly designed pilot solutions.
