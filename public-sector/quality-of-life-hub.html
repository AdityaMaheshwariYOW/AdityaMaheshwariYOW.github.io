Quality of Life Hub

A user-facing platform transforming Canadian well-being data into real-time insights for policymakers and citizens alike.

Executive Summary

  • Doubled user engagement year-over-year by curating personalized dashboards from survey & administrative data.
  • Halved operational overhead by automating report generation and implementing templated content workflows.
  • Launched three interdepartmental modernization initiatives, reducing time-to-insight and improving policy responsiveness.

Technical Overview

  • Data Integration: Consolidated multi-source datasets (surveys, tax records, health stats) via nightly ETL in R with Git-backed workflows.
  • Dashboarding & UX: Built interactive PowerBI reports with custom JS/CSS components and Adobe Analytics for user-event tracking.
  • Automation & CI/CD: Deployed RMarkdown pipelines and cron jobs, with version control via Git for reproducibility.
