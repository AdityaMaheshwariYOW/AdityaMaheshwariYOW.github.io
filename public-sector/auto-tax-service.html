Auto-Tax Navigator

Prototyped a CRA service to auto-file taxes for Ontario social assistance recipients by leveraging T5007 slip data—identifying underserved non-filers and streamlining benefit receipt.

Executive Summary

  • Identified 287,759 Ontario social assistance non-filers in 2021, up from 81,471 in 2018, using T5007 slip data.
  • Reduced barriers by automating tax filing for 200K+ recipients, improving access to benefits and credits.
  • Enabled CRA to target outreach and design low-touch engagement strategies for vulnerable populations.

Technical Overview

  • Data Acquisition: Ingested provincial T5007 slip files for OntarioWorks, TCAP, and ODSP; standardized fields across provinces.
  • Data Analysis: Mapped eligibility criteria (age, residency, asset limits) to slip records; computed non-filer counts by year and province.
  • Visualization & Delivery: Created R-powered dashboards with time-series and province breakdowns; compiled interactive tables for policy teams.
