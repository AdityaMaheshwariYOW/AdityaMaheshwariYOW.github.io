Inbox AI Assistant

At the Privy Council Office, I built a scalable AI pipeline to parse ~10,000 daily emails to the Prime Minister—transforming raw messages into actionable insights for government communications.

Executive Summary

  • Processed and classified ~10,000 PMO emails per day into key topics and sentiment trends, accelerating policy insights.
  • Presented outcomes at Code for America Summit 2024, demonstrating government-grade AI for public engagement analysis.
  • Established a reusable, scalable infrastructure blueprint for future federal ML projects.

Technical Overview

  • Data Pipeline: Automated secure email ingestion via Azure Functions and Databricks, cleansing and normalizing text with Spark.
  • Modeling: Implemented BERTopic for dynamic topic modeling and fine-tuned Hugging Face transformers for sentiment classification.
  • Deployment & Visualization: Deployed inference services in Azure ML Pipelines; visualized results in PowerBI with custom alerts and dashboards.
