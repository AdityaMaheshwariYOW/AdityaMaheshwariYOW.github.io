Research

School taught me the power of structured inquiry—so I continued taking courses and working as a Research Assistant to deepen my insights. Here are select projects where I bridged theory and practice.

CAMFED Impact Study

Evaluated the long-term effects of girls’ educational programs in Sub-Saharan Africa, combining causal inference with survey data to guide non-profit strategy.

Health Feedback Mining

Applied topic modelling to open-text responses in Alberta Health surveys—surfacing key themes in patient feedback to inform public health policy.

RL Traffic Control

In my Master’s thesis, deployed Deep Reinforcement Learning to optimize traffic flow on highway networks—demonstrating significant congestion reduction in simulations.

EI Claim Dynamics

Modeled Employment Insurance uptake patterns using administrative data—identifying determinants of claim duration and policy implications during economic downturns.

Rupee Demonetization Economics

Assessed India's 2016 demonetization impact using causal forests on transaction data—uncovering varied effects across regions and income groups.

Scholarly Contributions

Co-authored book chapters on data-driven policymaking and developmental economics—translating complex methodologies into actionable frameworks.
